About Conference

International conference on next generation computing systems

The International Conference on Next Generation Computing Systems is one of the flagship conferences of TOSKA, dedicated to young researchers and renowned scientists to come together and share their ideas about disruptions and future directions. Around 3,000 researchers are expected to submit proposals for paper presentations and program sessions to be held at the annual conference. After double blind review, technical papers, tutorials, workshops proposals are accepted. The 3 day event showcases several keynote speeches by distinguished speakers from industry, academia and government sectors; technical sessions featuring technical papers extensively reviewed by peers; workshops focusing on the latest trends in various technology; tutorials delivered by experts in respective disciplines; exhibits. ICNGCS is to be held in Gods own Country, Kerala in Cochin. With plenty of renowned academic institutions and industries around and an attraction for tourist, Cochin provides the best spot to be around.
